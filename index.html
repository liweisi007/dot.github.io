爱护和平

Cherish Peace • djkyc

和平不是一句口号，而是每个人内心深处的选择。
愿战火止息，愿分歧归于理解，愿世界充满爱。

Peace is not just a slogan, but a choice we make deep inside.
May the fighting cease, may differences turn into understanding, and may the world be filled with love.

一片橄榄叶，一份希望。让我们从自身做起，
用善意化解冲突，用合作替代对抗。

An olive branch carries hope. Let us start from ourselves,
resolving conflicts with kindness and replacing confrontation with cooperation.

科技应以人为本，和平亦需智慧守护。
我是一名开发者，愿用代码编织连接，而不是隔阂。

Technology should serve humanity, and peace needs wisdom to sustain.
As a developer, I build connections with code, not divisions.
